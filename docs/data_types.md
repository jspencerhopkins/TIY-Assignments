### `Numbers`
* **What does it mean?** numerical figures - integers, floats, etc
* **How do you make one?**  type it in
* **Why would you want one of those?** add, subtract, multiply, divide
* **What can you do with a number?**
```ruby
  # You can add:
  2 + 2 #=> 4
  
  # You can subtract:
  4 - 2 #=> 2
  
  # You can multiply:
  4 * 4 = 16
  
  # You can divide:
  4 / 2 = 2
```
